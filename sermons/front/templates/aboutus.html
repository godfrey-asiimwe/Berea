BEREA BAPTIST CHURCH. BUKOTO – KAMPALA

Berea Baptist Church was established in 2007 to model a return to diligent study of God’s Word, and an authentic preaching that is expository in content and passionate in delivery, enabling a full expression of biblical faith in practical Christian living. Some churches have convinced themselves that program, style, and catchy campaigns are more compelling or powerful than ministry centered on traditional preaching and teaching. But a church is not a corporation in need of a fresh message, a new promotional strategy, or different direction. The Bible is not silent on the course churches should follow, or the disaster that results from veering off that course. God has purposed that his church draws a life of blessing, stability, and power from being Scripture-driven.

Berea’s effort is directed at correcting such mistakes in contemporary ministry that are human centered in worship, preaching, spirituality and faith expression. Thus, we marshal our efforts at Berea with intentional commitment to the prayerful pursuit of God’s renewing grace in reformation and revival.

The Cause for Berea The most influential false doctrines that ever threatened the church have always masqueraded as genuine, employing the familiar language of Scripture but skewing the truth. Using the language of biblical Christianity is not necessarily the same as being biblical, This situation is not unique to our present time and culture; it is a pattern that has expressed itself in nearly every generation.

Preaching for Transformation

Corporate Worship at Berea

At Berea we acknowledge that in the biblical framework, worship is better described than defined.

But we affirm that “worship” is the believer’s response of all that they are — mind, emotions, will, body — to what God is, says and does. Worship is the submission of all our nature to “Worship” is the quickening of conscience by His holiness; the nourishment of mind with His truth; the purifying of imagination by His Beauty; the opening of the heart to His love; the surrender of will to His ‘purpose — and all of this gathered up in adoration, the most selfless emotion of which our nature is capable.

When we gather together, we focus our attention on God, giving Him our praise and receiving his blessing and instruction. We aim at “worship” that is focused upon the majesty and beauty of the Triune God, shaped by His self-revelation in Scripture, and borne in our daily lives.

Prayer at Berea

At Berea, prayer is the heartbeat of personal and church life.

We handle prayer as a unique, biblical and apostolic priority in Christian living and service, applying it as the foundation, the fabric and force of life and ministry. Prayer is the privilege God has given to us, to cooperate with him in his purpose, plan and will. And we affirm that no one can be greater than his / her prayer life. We teach and remind all people that prayer comes to us not only as a gift, but as a pattern after our Lord Jesus, and as a command for all who believe.
