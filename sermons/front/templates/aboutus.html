Our Identity

We are a congregation that seeks to honor God and to display His glory through the faithful preaching of his Word, thus bringing the reality of his nature and work among his people for a meaningful and consistent life of worship.

Berea Baptist Church exists to glorify God by making disciples who are:

  • developing in their relationship with God through worship.
  • increasing participation in the church through fellowship, and
  • involved in the world through witness.
